Caring on Call

A care manager in your corner — before you need one.

For people who like to plan ahead

Plan for your own aging — proactively, not in crisis.

Most families come to us in the middle of a crisis. Caring on Call is for people who want to do it differently — to put a professional in their corner before a health change forces tough decisions.

You're paired with a care manager who learns your wishes now, checks in regularly, and is ready to scale up support exactly when you need it.

What's Included

Your Caring on Call benefits

Your assigned care manager

A consistent professional who knows you, your wishes, and your history.

Initial in-home assessment

A thorough first visit that captures your situation, goals, and home setup.

Annual reassessments

Yearly visits to update your plan as your needs and circumstances evolve.

Quarterly check-ins

Regular calls to catch changes early and stay current with your life.

Guidance on options

Trusted advice when you face decisions about health, housing, or services.

Ready to scale

When your needs change, your care manager is already on board to expand support immediately.

Questions Caring on Call Helps You Answer

If any of these are on your mind, this is for you.

  • Can I stay in my home as I age?
  • If I move, what are my best options?
  • How do I balance my and my spouse's different needs?
  • When I need help, what's actually available to me?
  • How do I build a support network as a solo ager?
